# Quick Start

Get up and running with Deep Outbound in under 10 minutes.

## Step 1: Sign Up

1. Navigate to the login page
2. Click **Sign Up** and create an account with your email
3. Verify your email address

## Step 2: Add Your First Domain

1. Go to **Domains** in the sidebar
2. Click **Add Domain**
3. Enter your domain details:
   - **Domain name**: e.g., `weatherrobots`
   - **TLD**: e.g., `com`
   - **Buy Now Price**: Your asking price
   - **Landing Page URL**: Your Spaceship.com or other landing page

## Step 3: Find Potential Buyers

1. Click the action menu (â‹®) on your domain
2. Select **Find Leads**
3. Choose a search strategy:
   - **Domain Upgrade** (recommended for .com domains)
4. Wait for the search to complete
5. Select companies to scrape for contact info
6. Click **Scrape Sites**

## Step 4: Create an Email Template

1. Go to **Templates** in the sidebar
2. Click **New Email Template**
3. Select a default template to start with:
   - **Initial Outreach** - Friendly first contact
4. Customize the template with your messaging
5. Save the template

## Step 5: Launch a Campaign

1. Go to **Campaigns** in the sidebar
2. Click **New Campaign**
3. Follow the wizard:
   - Name your campaign
   - Add email steps with your template
   - Select your new leads
4. Click **Start Campaign**
